The ReVe Festival:
Finale

The ReVe Festival: Finale is Red Velvet's first compilation album, released on December 23, 2019. It is the third and final release of the trilogy The ReVe Festival. The album contains sixteen tracks including the lead single Psycho and three other new tracks. It also includes all tracks from The ReVe Festival: Day 1 and The ReVe Festival: Day 2. A digital EP including only the four new tracks was also released on the same date.
